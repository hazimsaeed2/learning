# EPAT Practical Trading Systems

*From Market Microstructure to Research, Risk, Machine Learning, Options, and Live Execution*

This book takes you from zero knowledge to practical mastery of systematic trading. It assumes nothing beyond everyday life and basic arithmetic: every idea is taught from scratch with plain English, real numbers, worked examples, figures, and hands-on practice notebooks. Expert depth lives in each chapter's Appendix — Going Deeper. Each chapter ends with a Master Recap you can reread a year later to restore the whole chapter in minutes.
